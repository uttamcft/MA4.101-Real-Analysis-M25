Welcome to Real Analysis M25

Real Analysis focuses on the rigorous foundation of calculus and its applications in mathematics. This course will cover sequences, series, limits, continuity, differentiability, and integrability. The aim is to develop a deeper understanding of mathematical analysis and prepare students for advanced courses. Detailed examples and exercises will be provided to build strong problem-solving skills. We also include topics like metric spaces and functional analysis basics. Each concept will be explained with proofs and applications. Students are expected to participate actively in class discussions. Office hours and extra help sessions will be arranged as needed.
