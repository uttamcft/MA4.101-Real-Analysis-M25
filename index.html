Real Analysis M25

Prerequisite

Elementary knowledge of Calculus is essential. Real Analysis provides the rigorous foundation...

Much of mathematics relies on solving equations, if not in explicit exact forms, then at least in establishing the existence of solutions. Real Analysis develops this material systematically in the context of real-valued functions of a real variable.

Detailed Syllabus

Units cover sequences, series, differential equations, and complex analysis basics.
